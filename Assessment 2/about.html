About 100SMILES Dental Care

100SMILES Dental Care is a modern dental care facility located at 33 Fantail Street in Razole. Our roots in the community run deep, with over three decades at this location and more than 50 years of experience in dental care overall.

We pride ourselves on offering friendly, reliable, and compassionate services. Our team provides a comprehensive range of dental care, from routine check-ups and cleanings to dental implants, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, root canal treatment and emergency care.

Our experienced dentists are well-equipped to handle common dental issues, offering diagnostics, treatments and surgeries when necessary. We also prioritise patient education so that you have the knowledge and resources to make informed decisions about your dental health, including nutritional guidance and oral hygiene tips.

100SMILES Dental Care is also thrilled to offer a convenient online booking system so you can schedule appointments anytime. Alternatively, you are always welcome to give us a call. We are currently running a new callout free trial for Dental Check-up for our valued clients to help you get started on your journey to a healthier smile.

At 100SMILES Dental Care, our promise is to deliver the highest standard of care for patients of all ages and dental needs. Our dedicated team of dentists and support staff share a strong commitment to dental health and to providing the attention and treatment you deserve.

Our New Dental Technologies

We are committed to staying at the forefront of dental technology to provide our patients with the best possible care. Our recently integrated technologies include:

  • Digital X-Rays - High-resolution images with significantly less radiation than traditional X-rays for quicker, more accurate diagnoses.
  • Intraoral Cameras - Detailed images of your teeth and gums that support early detection and clear explanation of treatment options.
  • Laser Dentistry - Minimally invasive procedures with reduced pain and faster healing, used for gum therapy, cavity detection and whitening.
  • CAD/CAM Technology - Same-visit, custom restorations such as crowns, bridges and veneers with a precise fit and natural appearance.
  • 3D Printing - Accurate dental models, surgical guides and custom appliances for more precise treatment planning.
  • Teledentistry - Virtual consultations that allow us to assess concerns and provide advice without an in-office visit.
